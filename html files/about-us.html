ABOUT

We measure success by the relationships that we build.

We set out to be different from other traditional real estate firms in Charleston right from the beginning. First, as real estate investors, building a real estate portfolio of our own of homes on the downtown peninsula. We studied the market intensely, became experts at market value, opportunity, and investment potential in a growing Charleston real estate market.

Next, we founded a construction company to keep costs in control and deliver uncompromising customer service. We added a real estate brokerage in order to share our expertise with clients seeking a home in Charleston and we launched a vacation rental management company after our clients asked for a way to continue their relationship with us. All of this designed with only one thing in mind - to be a singular resource for our clients, and to deliver an exceptional experience for buyers in the Charleston real estate market.

Mission Statement

Mike Schaap Builders, Inc. exists to provide exceptional workmanship through the quality and personal ownership of our product and services; to bring value to clients through attentive details, professional integrity, and aggressive performance; and to lead with character and purpose that brings honor to God.

EXCELLENCE

Demonstrated by our precise attention to detail, thorough processes, and an unwillingness to compromise on the quality of our work. As each member of our team brings their expertise and passion to their role, we strive to exceed customer expectations.

INTEGRITY

Demonstrated by our commitment to fairness, respect, and honesty to one another and our clients. We prioritize doing the right thing and in doing so, honor our commitments and one another.

INNOVATIVE & CREATIVE

Demonstrated by our passion to learn and know the desires of each client and their individual vision. We challenge ourselves and one another to be continual learners, problem solvers, change embracers, and fresh creators.

TEAMWORK

Demonstrated by the value and honor we give to one another. Because we believe in the strengths of each individual and the gifts they bring to the team, we strive to create a space that provides collaboration, satisfaction, and celebration. We esteem each team member as our work family, and we value the personal family of each team member.

COMMUNICATION

Demonstrated by a desire to bring clarity, kindness, and solutions in our conversations. We strive to ask questions, listen generously, and communicate clearly in our interactions with our team and clients. We believe that the quality of our communication is foundational to the growth and success of every aspect of our roles.
